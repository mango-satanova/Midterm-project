Genres: Adventure, Action, and Science Fiction.
Topics: shonen, superpower, and school.
Years of issue: 2016, 2017, 2018, 2019, 2021.
Original title: Boku no Hero Academia

In the world of "Boku no Hero Academia" everyone can become a superhero,
because people are immediately born with an unusual power called a Quirk.
Everyone except Midoriya - a boy who dreams of becoming a superhero,
perhaps more than many others, but by an unfortunate accident born
without a Quirk. And, as usually happens in anime, the case again decides
everything: the most famous and strongest superhero in the world,
nicknamed the Almighty, takes to train Midoriya,
after which he gives him part of his strength so that he can fulfill his dream -
he entered the superhero academy .
