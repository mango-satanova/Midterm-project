all Might

BIRTHDAY: 6/10

Individuality: one for all

The No. 1 hero, also known as the symbol of peace.
He was seriously injured in a battle in the past and can
only act as a hero for a limited time due to the aftereffects.
He has a "personality" one for all that can be transferred by
accumulating the power that has been passed down from generation
to generation, and gave it to him for a long time.
